About the Role

We are looking for NV1 or NV2 ICT Administrator to support the design, implementation, and operation of observability systems within a Defence environment. The role is responsible for deploying and maintaining monitoring tools, with a primary focus on Elastic Stack and OpenText Operations Bridge Manager (OBM) to provide real-time observability across IT infrastructure and applications.




Responsibilities and Accountabilities:

Design, implement, and maintain observability systems using Elastic Stack and OBM.
Develop procedures and tools to enhance the management and support of monitoring environments.
Set up and configure Elasticsearch, Logstash, Kibana, and Operations Bridge Manager (OBM), including component installation and integration.
Collect, analyze, and visualize metrics, logs, traces, and events from various systems to ensure full-stack observability.
Design and maintain dashboards, alerts, and reports tailored for IT operations, development, and security teams.
Monitor system performance proactively, identify bottlenecks, and optimize configurations for efficiency and scalability.
Provide technical support, issue resolution, and collaborate with other teams for continuous improvement.
Implement and manage security configurations including authentication, authorization, and encryption.
Maintain detailed documentation on configurations, procedures, and troubleshooting steps.
Stay current with trends, features, and best practices in monitoring and observability technologies.






Requirements and Experience:

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, or a related field (or equivalent experience).
Proven experience in a Monitoring and Observability-focused ICT role.
Hands-on experience with Elastic Stack (Elasticsearch, Logstash, Kibana) and Operations Bridge Manager.
Strong understanding of IT infrastructure (Linux, Windows, networks, cloud platforms like Azure, etc.).
Scripting and automation skills (Python, Bash, Ansible, etc.).
Strong analytical and troubleshooting capabilities.
Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
Primary work location is Mitchell, ACT.
Occasional access to other Defence sites within Canberra may be required.
Remote work is subject to prior approval by the Director.
Standard business hours apply: 08:00 – 18:00 AET.


Security Clearance:

Must have NV1 or ability to obtain NV2 security clearance.
