Join Our Cybersecurity Team – Operational Technology (OT) Focus

Are you passionate about securing critical infrastructure and industrial systems? We’re looking for a skilled and driven Cybersecurity Specialist (OT Focus) to join our team and play a key role in protecting our operational technology environments.

 

About the Role

As part of our growing cybersecurity team, you’ll focus on the unique challenges of OT/ICS (Industrial Control Systems) security. From network segmentation and threat detection to policy enforcement and incident response, you’ll help secure the technology that keeps our operations running.

This is an exciting opportunity to work in a high-impact role with cross-functional teams, making a real difference in our organisation’s cyber resilience.

 

Key Responsibilities

Assess, design, and implement security controls for OT environments (SCADA, DCS, PLCs, HMIs).

Conduct risk assessments and vulnerability scans in OT networks.

Collaborate with various stakeholders to align security practices.

Monitor OT security systems, investigate anomalies, and lead incident response.

Ensure compliance with relevant standards and regulations (e.g., IEC 62443, NERC CIP, ISO/ISA 27001).

 

Required Qualifications

Education: Bachelor's degree in Cybersecurity, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or a related field.

Experience:

3–5+ years in cybersecurity with at least 2 years in OT/ICS environments.

Experience with industrial network protocols (Modbus, DNP3, OPC, etc.) and architectures.

Familiarity with threat modeling and risk management in OT settings.

 

Preferred Certifications

Desirable:

ISA/IEC 62443 Cybersecurity Certificate(s)

Offensive Security Certified Professional (OSCP) or equivalent penetration testing certification.

Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP)

Certified Information Security Manager (CISM)

CompTIA Security+, Network+, or CySA+
